Derbyshire Dales

Derbyshire Dales (E07000035) is a local authority district in Derbyshire. The district's population of 71,116 at the time of the 2011 census made it the 309th largest in England and Wales. Derbyshire Dales saw a population increase of 2.4% from 2001.
