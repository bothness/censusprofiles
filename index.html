This prototype is made using historical census datasets covering areas in England and Wales from 1981 to 2011 (1, 2, 3, 4), and geographic data from the ONS Open Geography Portal. It will be updated on 28 June to include the latest 2021 Census data. Population counts should match published ONS datasets, but percentage figures are not official.

This project is a work in progress by the ONS digital content team, and may not lead to a final product. If you have any comments or suggestions please share them here, or send a tweet/DM to project lead Ahmad Barclay.
