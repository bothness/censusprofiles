Basingstoke and Deane

Basingstoke and Deane (E07000084) is a local authority district in Hampshire. The district's population of 167,799 at the time of the 2011 census made it the 114th largest in England and Wales. Basingstoke and Deane saw a population increase of 10.0% from 2001.
