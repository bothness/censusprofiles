Merthyr Tydfil

Merthyr Tydfil (W06000024) is a local authority district in Wales. The district's population of 58,802 at the time of the 2011 census made it the 320th largest in England and Wales. Merthyr Tydfil saw a population increase of 5.0% from 2001.
