King's Lynn and West Norfolk

King's Lynn and West Norfolk (E07000146) is a local authority district in Norfolk. The district's population of 154,300 at the time of the 2021 census made it the 142nd largest in England and Wales. King's Lynn and West Norfolk saw a population increase of 4.6% from 2011.
